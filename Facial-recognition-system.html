Facial Recognition System

In today's world, a building's security measures often include a biometric facial recognition and identification system. Now that face recognition programmes can detect and recognise facial dynamics with near-perfect accuracy in settings as varied as convenience stores, airports, and train stations, they are rapidly becoming an integral part of our daily lives. Fusion Apps' Facesense is a scalable facial identification and recognition solution built on a neural computing platform that serves a wide range of industries and uses.

Working Process

1
Instant identification

Facesense microsecond facial identification is the difference in authentication, preventing crimes, and saving lives because it uses the industry's most stringent detection parameters for facial identity.

2
Realtime alerts

With Facesense's network-wide integration, you'll never miss an alert. Real-time alerts can be set up to notify you via a centralised dashboard, a distributed dashboard, an app, an email, or an SMS whenever a visitor or someone of interest enters your desired area of control.

3
Distributed environments

Facesense operates well in environments with multiple nodes, databases, platforms, and networks. Facesense ensures maximum efficiency by centralising previously dispersed systems for complicated detection and identification.

1
Anonymous cataloguing

Automated indexing of non-recognised people or visitors with AI powered Intelli search tag helps our customers generate vast database with intelligent search & purge options when needed.

2
Scalable network

Facesense's mobile scalability is unparalleled and among the best in the market, whether you have a single camera or hundreds of cameras in a dispersed network. You may easily scale up or down on the go without the headaches of complex infrastructure by using the plug-in features on our streamlined control panel.

3
Encrypted data security

With encryptions and safeguards built to international standards for data security like ISO27001, Facesense is designed to keep your data safe from the get-go.

1
Integrated centralised dashboard

While we handle the complexities, our customers get a simple, easy-to-use, and powerful tool to manage facial recognition system installations of any size. Facesense dashboard's upgraded GUI and lightweight architecture helps you monitor, track, edit, scale-up & down, and manage numerous locations simply.

2
Intelli search

We provide the greatest security systems with our cutting-edge AI tools. Intelli Search's sophisticated AI algorithms enhance your face recognition system by automatically identifying and recognising wanted and anonymous visitors on search engines.

3
Geo tracking

Our cooperative networks use Facesense to track suspects across the city. This programme locates and alerts suspects across the city.

3
Open networks

When using Facesense, it doesn't matter what kind of camera you have. Our facial recognition software was developed on a framework that allows it to communicate with devices from a wide variety of manufacturers.
